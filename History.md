## v0.1.0

- Features:
    - Layer support:
        - GeoJSON layer.
        - Tiled WMS layer.
        - XYZ layer.
        - TMS layer (using XYZ).
    - Layer grouping.
    - Automatic layer projection alignment.

        When a layer is added to a map, its projection will automatically align with the map projection.
